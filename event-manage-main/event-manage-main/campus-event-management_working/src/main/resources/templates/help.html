❓ Need Help?

Welcome to the College Event Management Support Center.

How to Join an Event?

Login to your student dashboard, view the available events, and hit the 'Interested? Join Event' button. It will instantly enroll you.

Can I leave an Event?

Currently, enrollment is final to guarantee headcount for food and seating. Please contact the Admin directly if you registered by mistake.

I forgot my password!

Please shoot an email to admin@college.edu or visit the main office on campus to have your credentials reset manually.
